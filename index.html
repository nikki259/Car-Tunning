Our History

Garage Simulator has been a family owned business since 2001. We opened up for business in March of 2001 exclusively selling wheels at out first location on Miami Blvd. in Durham, NC.

A few years later we added audio/video to our services. Next we added in-house custom interior and minor mechanic work. In 2007 we moved to our Glenwood Ave location. With the larger location we were able to add painting in 2010. Now we are one of the few locations in the triangle that has all these services in-house. We have clients from parents who want to add a back-up camera to a vehicle to enhance safety to their children’s car to professional athletes who are customizing every aspect of the vehicle.

Let our experienced, knowledgeable and courteous staff help make your visit an efficient and economical one.
